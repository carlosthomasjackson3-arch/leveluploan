How it works

The website version keeps the same structure you wanted from the app, but packaged as a stronger browser experience.

1. Apply

Borrower enters core info and explains why they need the loan.

2. Train

Borrower completes short tests, recall prompts, and definitions.

3. Agree

Borrower reviews the agreement center and confirms understanding.

4. Review

The final step reinforces seriousness and sends the borrower into your intake handoff.

5. Admin

Applications can be reviewed manually, escalated, or sent for more info.

6. Repay

Approved borrowers can be shown a repayment page and then handled manually through secure payment links.
